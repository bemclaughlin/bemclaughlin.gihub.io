TABOO TOPICS IN ELT PUBLISHING

POLITICS Avoid politics

Avoid images and discussions of politics, politicians, or political systems or parties. You should also avoid overtly expressing political opinions or baiases and advocating for or against political positions.
ALCOHOL Avoid images of alcohol or drinking

Avoid images or discussions of drinking or alcohol. Pay particular attention to photographs of parties or social events, as these often feature images of wine or beer.
RELIGION Avoid religious imagery

Avoid discussions, imagery, or symbolism associated with religion. You should also avoid expressing religious beliefs and advocating for or against a specific religion.
SEX Avoid sexual imagery

Avoid images or discussions of sexual activity. You should avoid images that may be perceived as romantic or sexual. This includes members of the opposite sex touching. It also includes images of nudity or people in revealing clothing.
NARCOTICS Avoid depictions of drug use

Avoid images or discussions of illicit drugs or drug use. This also includes images of people smoking cigarettes.
ISMS Avoid -isms

Avoid images or discussions that portray sexist, racist, ablist, agist, or sectarian attitudes. Evaluate your content carefully to ensure that you avoid apparent biases. Also avoid discussions of social systems or ideologies, such as communism, authoritarianism, marxism, etc.
PORK Avoid pork products

Avoid images of pork products, including ham and bacon. In particular, check photographs of meals carefully, as they can often include images of pork products.
